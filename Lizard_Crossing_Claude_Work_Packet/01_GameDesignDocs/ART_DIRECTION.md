# ART DIRECTION

## Target style
Stylized-realistic, premium mobile 3D.

Not fully realistic horror.
Not flat toy prototype.
Not low-effort default Unity shapes.

The look should be:
- colorful
- tropical
- readable
- polished
- bright in daytime levels
- cinematic in night/rain levels
- kid-friendly but exciting

## Camera composition
The lizard is near the bottom center of the screen, moving forward.
The safe zone is visible ahead.
Hazards cross sideways between the lizard and the safe zone.

## Composition rule
The screenshot should instantly communicate:
tiny lizard -> giant sideways hazards -> safe zone ahead.

## Environment themes
1. Garden Escape - colorful tropical alley with flowers and potted plants.
2. Boardwalk Rush - beach boardwalk, scooter wheels, tourists.
3. Curb Gauntlet - curb, drain, cars, tires, crosswalk.
4. Midnight Dash - rainy neon alley, boots, reflections.
5. Patio Panic - café patio, chair legs, crumbs, sandals, sneakers.

## Important visual details
- detailed pavement texture
- cracks, pebbles, leaves, gum, crumbs
- red warning circles or shadows before hazards land
- giant shoe soles close to camera
- bright readable safe zone sign
- simple clean HUD
- strong depth and scale

## What NOT to copy from early prototype
- empty flat spaces
- cone-only obstacles
- high detached camera
- normal third-person scale
- hazards walking forward with the lizard
- bland pastel scene with no danger
- unclear goal
