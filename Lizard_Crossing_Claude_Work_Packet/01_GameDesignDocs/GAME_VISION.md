# GAME VISION

## Title
Lizard Crossing

## One-sentence pitch
A tiny lizard tries to survive giant human sidewalks in short, level-based crossing challenges.

## Genre
Mobile 3D arcade crossing / runner hybrid.

## Inspiration
Crossy Road-style crossing logic, but fully revamped:
- low cinematic lizard POV
- higher graphical quality
- level-based missions
- giant sideways-moving human hazards
- collectible lizards and cosmetics later

## Core loop
Pick lizard -> start level -> move forward -> dodge sideways hazards -> collect bugs -> reach safe zone -> earn stars/rewards -> unlock progression.

## Player fantasy
"I am a tiny fast lizard surviving a giant human world."

## Must-have feel
- small
- fast
- vulnerable
- playful
- cinematic
- readable
- satisfying near-misses

## What makes the game special
Not just a lizard moving forward.
The special thing is the scale fantasy:
- massive sneakers cross the path
- sidewalk cracks look like trenches
- scooter wheels feel like rolling walls
- car tires feel terrifying
- the safe zone feels like survival
